Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2024

This Privacy Policy describes how Simple Inventory Planner ("the Add-on") collects, uses, and protects information when you use the Google Sheets add-on.

1. Information We Collect

The Add-on accesses only the data necessary to provide its functionality:

  • Spreadsheet data — inventory figures, product names, and quantities you enter in your Google Sheet.
  • User settings — thresholds and preferences you configure, stored via Google Apps Script PropertiesService.
  • Email address — used solely to send low-stock alert emails that you explicitly configure.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To calculate replenishment recommendations based on your spreadsheet data.
  • To send automated email alerts when stock levels fall below your defined thresholds.
  • To save and restore your preferences between sessions.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond the core features of the Add-on.

3. Data Storage

All data processed by the Add-on remains within Google's infrastructure (Google Apps Script and Google Sheets). No data is transmitted to external servers or third parties. Settings are stored using Google's PropertiesService and are accessible only within your Google account.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your data with any third parties. Your spreadsheet data is never sent outside of Google's platform.

5. Permissions Explained

  • spreadsheets.currentonly — read/write the active spreadsheet only.
  • script.storage — save your settings and configuration.
  • script.container.ui — display the sidebar panel inside Sheets.
  • script.send_mail — send alert emails on your behalf.
  • script.scriptapp — create scheduled triggers to run automated checks.

6. Data Retention

Stored settings are retained until you uninstall the Add-on or manually clear them. Uninstalling the Add-on removes all associated stored properties.

7. Children's Privacy

The Add-on is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.
