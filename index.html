What it does

Simple Inventory Planner is a Google Sheets add-on that helps e-commerce sellers and inventory managers calculate reorder quantities, set safety stock levels, and receive automated email alerts before stockouts happen. Point it to your existing Sales, SOH, and Aging sheets — no reformatting needed.

Features

Works With Your Existing Sheets

Point to any sheet in your spreadsheet for Sales, Stock on Hand, and Aging data. Select which columns contain SKU, quantity, and aging days — no fixed format required.

Replenishment Planning

Automatically calculates Reorder Point, Safety Stock, Coverage Days, and Suggested Reorder Quantity for every SKU based on sales velocity and lead time.

SKU Filter

Filter analysis by brand, category, or any column value — or supply a custom SKU list from another sheet. Analyse only the products you need.

Per-Category Settings

Set different lead times, safety stock days, and coverage targets for different product categories or brands. One tool handles your entire portfolio.

Aging Stock Detection

Link to your aging report to automatically flag slow-moving and aged inventory. STATUS shows OOS, Reorder, Aging, Overstock, Low Stock, or Normal.

Email Alerts & Scheduled Reports

Receive automated email notifications when inventory drops below your thresholds. Set up daily or weekly scheduled checks so you never miss a stockout.

Permissions used

  • Google Sheets (current spreadsheet only) — read and write data in the active spreadsheet to perform inventory analysis
  • Script Storage — save your settings and column mapping preferences
  • UI — display the settings sidebar panel inside Sheets
  • Email (Send Mail) — send you low-stock alert emails when inventory falls below thresholds
  • Triggers (ScriptApp) — schedule automated inventory checks on your behalf
